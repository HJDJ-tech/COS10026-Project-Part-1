Data Analyst

About the Role

We are looking for a skilled Data Analyst to join our tech company. The role involves analyzing large datasets, providing insights, and supporting cross-functional teams with data-driven recommendations. The ideal candidate will have strong data analysis skills and the ability to communicate findings clearly.

Responsibilities:

  • Manage network-related projects, including design, planning, execution, and handover to Operations.
  • Manage project timelines, identify and resolve issues while ensuring minimal impact on BAU operations.
  • Adhere to network policies, procedures, and security best practices during project deployments.
  • Collaborate with stakeholders to analyse customer needs and translate them into effective network solutions.

Qualifications and Skills:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field.
  • 2+ years of data analysis experience.
  • Experience with data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau, Power BI).
  • Proficiency in SQL.

Salary and Benefits

  • Salary range: $90,000 - $105,000
  • 6 weeks of PTO
  • Super: 13.75%
